The Global Network

Held at one of the most prestigious medieval properties in the world, the Castle Gathering is a non-profit, invitation-only, fully-curated experience inspiring a new network of alliances. Treasured guests from all around the world are invited to gather once a year in Tuscany, Italy where we inspire deep connections through culture and conscious activities. Our event and community are oriented around three core principles: Truth, Compassion, and Beauty. Through these values we share ideas, resources, and perspectives to maximize impact.

About Us

The Castle Gathering is produced by Wellspring, a non-profit foundation that was established in 2018 by a collective of influential and purpose-driven individuals and organizations committed to scaling social impact.

The Castle

Castello di Segalari

Enjoy the 10th century medieval Castle, with its expansive views. Relax in the infinity pool, enjoy the mediterranean breeze, and allow yourself to be captivated by the charm of the Italian countryside.
