Who We Are

The Organizers

Simone, Walfredo, Giulia, and Grace are passionate visionaries enthusiastic about the future of technology and innovation. They conceived the Castle Gathering as an opportunity to convene some of the most creative and inventive minds working to envision and engineer our decentralized future. Long-time friends, Simone and Walfredo found a kindred spirit in Giulia, an advocate of using technology for the collective benefit of humankind. They shared a vision of hosting a high caliber Blockchain and Cryptocurrency event in an Italian castle reflecting the beauty and inspiration of Renaissance Italy. Grace joined their team as a brilliant ally and organizer to bring the dream to reality and usher in a great future grounded in truth, compassion, and beauty.

Simone Giacomelli

Founder, Vulpem Ventures

vulpem.com

Simone is a technologist with significant expertise in decentralised systems as it applies to the blockchain. He is the founder of Vulpem Ventures, an Italian-based cybersecurity and blockchain consultancy that manages security for more than $300M worth of blockchain assets.
Simone is also leading the blockchain development at SingularityNET, a global AI network that lets anyone create, share, and monetize AI services at scale.

Founder of The Opportunivore, Giulia fosters business opportunities by building bridges across people and countries and supports companies to conceive decentralized business models and supports them in the integration of Blockchain and immersive (e.g. VR/AR) technologies that can unlock Human Potential and generate Maximum Net Benefit, so using business as a force for collective Good.

Giulia Pische

Founder, The Opportunivore

theopportunivore.com

Walfredo della Gherardesca

Founder, LorenzoVinci.it

lorenzovinci.it

Italian entrepreneur active in the FoodTech and Food Distribution business. In late 2014 he founded LorenzoVinci.it which is today the most relevant ecosystem of small and local food manufacturers in Italy.
His life mission is to bring the European food industry towards a more sustainable future, while nurturing century-old traditions and protecting biodiversity.

As Healer, Visionary, and Teacher, Grace founded Spirit Guide Medicine and has served thousands of clients supporting their integral health and embodied well being.
She is now applying her skills to larger systems of humanity in concert with a dedicated and brilliant group of artists, technologists, and pioneers to help create a new set of causes for a regenerative, thriving future, facilitating humankind’s return and reunion to a life in integrity with all our relations.
